Welcome to the online FRAMEWORKS component!

PURPOSE
During this component you will be introduced to six research-based instructional strategies to assist you in the design and development of highly effective classroom lessons.

REQUIREMENTS
While this component is self paced you will need to:

  1. Have this entire module COMPLETED BEFORE the first night of face to face classes.

  2. On the first night, you will turn in completed assignments and participate in activities to demonstrate to the instructors that you have completed the module.

  3. There will be an Activity Summary Page for each module online.

  4. You will need to complete these activities and organize a Frameworks Notebook. In your Notebook you will insert:
    • the completed Activity Summary Page for each activity (There may be additional items that you will need to include. If so, those additional items will be listed on the Activity Summary Page.)
    • additional materials you may find that will help you in the classroom...please include them! (The more information you arm yourself with, the more prepared you will be when you enter the classroom.)

On your first night of institute, you will need to bring:

  • your completed Frameworks Notebook to class, organized and tabbed out by activity so you can find things quickly.

In the face to face components of the preparation institute you will:

  • observe our instructors model all of these strategies and demonstrate a practical approach to incorporating them into your lessons.

Resources Needed:
